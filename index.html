Lokate App

Lokate offers an innovative indoor location tracking system designed to revolutionize various business models. Our system provides valuable location data, enabling retailers to gain insights into customer habits. Using advanced machine learning models, we uncover not only conscious but also unconscious behaviors, like which store sections customers spend the most time in. This valuable data is then linked to specific products, empowering business owners with actionable analytics to boost their profits. Our user-friendly dashboard simplifies the decision-making process, making it easier for business owners to leverage this data for success.
